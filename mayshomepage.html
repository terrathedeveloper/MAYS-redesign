Our services reshape youth

Family Support

Sustainable change begins in the home. Our grassroots approach to lasting personal improvement assesses the needs of each family member and their impact on the whole family unit.

Afternoon Reporting

Recognizing that the peak hours for crime immediately follow school, we provide after-school reporting services to maintain focus on education and provide tutoring for students during times of struggle.

Gang Intervention

With a focus on social education, our intervention method breaks the generational ties to gang affiliation and provides a safe and secure environment for youth to learn about and engage in the community.

Day Reporting

We provide programming specifically designed to develop youth who struggle with communication skills and sustainable positive decision-making, remediating destructive habits into lasting integrity.

Community Coaching

Our deeply-rooted local partner network provides access to a wide range of resources to support youth and their families in the community, offering a village within which a child can build confidence.

Evening Reporting

The early hours of night offer another opportunity for idle minds to learn destructive habits. Our proactive evening reporting program provides a safe and secure environment for youth to socialize and grow.
